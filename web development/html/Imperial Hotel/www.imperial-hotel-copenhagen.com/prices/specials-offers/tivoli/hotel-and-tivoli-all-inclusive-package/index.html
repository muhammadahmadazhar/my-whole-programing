Tivoli All Inclusive

Tivoli All Inclusive – Enjoy the full package

To make your stay in Copenhagen as easy as possible, we have put everything together in one full package. With our package ‘Tivoli All Inclusive’, you will get a hotel stay, a trip to the amusement park Tivoli Gardens and a lovely dinner at one of several selected restaurants. 

The package includes

An overnight stay in one of our beautiful rooms
Delicious breakfast buffet including freshly baked bread, eggs and fruit
Entrance to the Tivoli Gardens*
An Unlimited Ride Ticket (up to 7 years) or Unlimited Ride Ticket Plus (8 years+) for the Tivoli Gardens amusements*
Virtual Reality in the Demon (only for Unlimited Ride Ticket Plus)*
One game at the Choco Wheel (only for Unlimited Ride Ticket Plus)*
Entrance to Tivoli’s Aquarium*
One Ride Photo / Balloon in memory of your fun day*
A meal at one of several selected restaurants within the Tivoli Gardens* 

*Only one time per person per stay. Kindly note that children under 3 years need a separate All Inclusive Ticket as this is not included in the price of your stay. The ticket may be purchased at the hotel upon arrival.
Price DKK 360.

*Also, kindly note that children up to 7 years of age are only allowed to receive and use the regular Unlimited Ride Ticket. As such, they are not allowed to use or upgrade to the Unlimited Ride Ticket Plus, as the Unlimited Ride Ticket Plus includes elements only intended for older children. For instance, you need to have a minimum height of 132cm in order to try the Demon and thereby Virtual Reality glasses. If the child is under 8 years old but taller than 132cm, the amusement supervisor will determine if the child may use Virtual Reality glasses. 

 

from DKK 805,-

Price per person for 2 adults and 2 children (3-7 y.o.)

More info about your All Inclusive stay

Where can I buy my Tivoli All Inclusive package?

Buy your Tivoli All Inclusive package directly on our website or call +45 3312 8000. 

How do I use my Tivoli All Inclusive package?

Upon arrival at the hotel, our reception will provide you with all the information you need on how to use your All Inclusive package in the Tivoli Gardens. 

Where can we enjoy the meal, and what can we choose between?

Many Tivoli restaurants and eateries offer adults’ and children’s menus that are included in your All Inclusive package. You will find something for every taste for both children and adults. 

You can redeem your meal voucher at all participating eateries featuring an All Inclusive logo on the door. 

Read more here. 

Table reservations are not possible with the All Inclusive package.  

Which amusements offer the Ride Photo voucher service?

Ride Photo vouchers can be used on the Vintage Cars, the Roller Coaster and the Demon. 

Use of the Unlimited Ride Ticket and Ride Photo voucher requires compliance with all security measures, and users must also possess tickets or Unlimited Ride Tickets for the amusements. Tivoli Gardens cannot be held responsible for photograph vouchers in cases of out-of-order amusements. 

When is Tivoli All Inclusive package valid?

Tivoli Gardens cannot be held responsible for possible closings of Tivoli’s Aquarium in connection with events at the Tivoli Concert Hall, etc. Nor can the Tivoli Gardens be held responsible for changes to menus, sold-out dishes, fully booked restaurants or out-of-order amusements. 

The package is not valid on 31 December 2018. The hotel cannot be held responsible for any changes in the package made by the Tivoli Gardens. 

Terms and conditions:

This offer is valid for a limited number of rooms, and the price shown is always per night. The price may vary from day to day. Some of the items in this offer applies once per person per stay only.
